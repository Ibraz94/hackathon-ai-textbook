# Module 1: Introduction to Robotics

This is the first module, introducing the fascinating world of robotics.

## What is a Robot?

A robot is an autonomous or semi-autonomous device that can perform tasks, often physical, with minimal human intervention.

### Types of Robots

There are many different types of robots, including:
*   Industrial robots
*   Humanoid robots
*   Mobile robots
*   Drones

## Historical Overview

Robotics has a rich history, evolving from early automatons to complex AI-powered machines.

## Applications of Robotics

Robots are used in a wide range of applications, such as manufacturing, healthcare, exploration, and domestic tasks.

### Robotics in Manufacturing

Robots automate repetitive and dangerous tasks in factories, improving efficiency and safety.

### Robotics in Healthcare

Robots assist in surgeries, dispense medication, and provide companionship.
